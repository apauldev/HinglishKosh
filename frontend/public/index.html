Why HinglishKosh?

Over 600 million people in India communicate daily in Hinglish — a natural blend of Hindi and English written in the Roman script. It's how you text your friends, type on WhatsApp, and scroll through social media.

Yet before this, there was no open dictionary for it. Academic resources use ISO 15919 transliteration (cāy, pānī) — accurate but nobody types that way. Commercial keyboards keep their dictionaries closed.

HinglishKosh fixes that.
